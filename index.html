The Course at General Assembly

I had been looking for a new path in my journey of life, when a friend mentioned GA. I attended a Info session, with some of the previous students and teachers that gave us details of what was in store. Once I had gone through interviews and done some of the work required, I was ready and set to tread in new territory. The real challange was learning to learn again.

What coding Languages did we work with?

As I had never coded before, and had basic knowledge in CSS3 and HTML5, everything looked scary.

We worked with Ruby and Sinatra, not the "New york, New york" we all know. There was Ruby on Rails, Javascript, SQL, PostgreSQL, JQuery, Json, Jekyll, AngularJS, AJAX and Heroku to name a few.The best part of all? That there's soo much more I want to learn.

Feelings and emotions after the 12 weeks...

It has been very hard and rewarding. There were days that I couldn't see the light but, with the help of teachers and fellow students it all became clear.

Is the Course with GA worth it? My honest answer is YES. You learn coding languages, and get a feel of what is it like to work as a team and be part of something great.

Take a look at some of the Projects I completed

Restaurant Recipe

My idea generated from my previous experience in restaurant trade. As there are so many big chains that mass produce food, I wanted to concentrate in smaller independent food joints. A app, that would allow the user to locate restaurats near, book or save the restaurants recipe.

Hacky Memory

24 hours to work on a project as part of a group. What would the outcome be? Because we wanted to do something interesting, and fun for the whole class to enjoy, we constructed a memory card game. Match the face of student or teacher to their catch phrase. Also the extra showreel of 'making off' is a bonus. Will u find it?

J&S Fitness

My second project was a group project. The feature of the app is to have a user sign up and do one of two things. Take a fitness class or teach a class. Depending on instructor/student we can book or cancel, and if the session was good, you can leave a comment.

DJ Tracks

As our first project I chose something that i was confortable with, so i chose the Music app. The idea was to upload music by new artists and have coments on the music, band or artists.
